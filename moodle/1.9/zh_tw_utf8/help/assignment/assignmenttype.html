作業型態

這裡有不同型態的作業

離線作業 - this is useful when the assignment is performed outside of Moodle. Students can see a description of the assignment, but can't upload files or anything. Teachers can grade all students though, and students will see notifications of their grades.

上傳一個檔案 - this type of assignment allows all students to upload a single file, of any type. This might be a Word processor document, or an image, a zipped web site, or anything you ask them to submit. Teachers can grade submitted assignments online.
